The Story of Monarch

Founded in 2017 by Martin Muyenga in Walvis Bay, Monarch Lifeguard & Emergency Rescue Services was born out of a critical need for professionalized aquatic safety in Namibia.

What started as a small team protecting local beaches has grown into Namibia's premier rescue authority. From the foggy shores of the Skeleton Coast to municipal pools in Windhoek, our mission remains the same: to prevent drownings through education, vigilance, and world-class rescue capabilities.
