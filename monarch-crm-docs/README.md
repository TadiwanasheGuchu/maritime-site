# Monarch Lifeguard CRM

> Internal management system for Monarch Lifeguard & Emergency Services — Walvis Bay, Namibia.

## What This System Does

A full CRM built to manage every part of the Monarch Lifeguard operation:

- Track clients (municipalities, companies, resorts)
- Manage trainee enrollments and issue certificates
- Schedule lifeguard deployments at beaches and pools
- Log incidents and emergency responses
- Generate quotes and invoices
- Export reports and PDF documents
